Privacy Policy

At Kim Tae Limited ("KimTae"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or purchase our products, in compliance with the **Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO)** of Hong Kong.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, including:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing address.
  • Order Information: Details about the products you purchase and your transaction history.
  • Payment Information: While we do not store full credit card numbers, we collect payment-related data necessary to process your transaction via our secure payment partners.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected via cookies for site optimization and security.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: To process, ship, and deliver your KimTae AI Translation Earphones.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance.
  • Direct Marketing: To send you promotions and updates (only if you have provided explicit opt-in consent).
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions or unauthorized access.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and fulfilling your orders, such as:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Shopify Payments, PayPal).
  • Shipping and logistics partners (e.g., DHL, FedEx).
  • Marketing and analytics platforms.

We ensure that all third parties respect the security of your personal data and treat it in accordance with the law.

5. Your Rights

Under the PDPO, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Object to the use of your data for direct marketing purposes.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, although disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.
